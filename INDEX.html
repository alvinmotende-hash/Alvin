About Me

Hello! My name is Alvin, and photography has been my passion since I first picked up a camera as a teenager. What began as a hobby quickly grew into a lifelong pursuit of capturing the world’s beauty through my lens. I specialize in portraits and landscapes, always striving to tell a story with each shot. For me, photography is not just about images—it’s about preserving emotions, memories, and moments that might otherwise be forgotten. This website is my way of sharing those stories with others.
